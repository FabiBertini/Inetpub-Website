About

Some information about our journey so far

  • 1990-1996

    First steps

    From Pascal and Cobol running a CP500 machine to Delphi with Paradox and Oracle databases.

  • 1997-1999

    Professional Realization

    The pleasure to make customers and business happy as ever before, implementing a bespoke CRM platform.

  • 2000-2005

    Distributed Applications and Web revolution

    Corba, Dcom, RPC and Java. Build bespoke frameworks and consolidation of SOLID principles and Clean architecture.

  • 2006-2017

    Design

    Thousands of applications bouncing inside an enterprise landscape. Strategic views combined with roadmaps can lead you to a successful business avoiding all pitfalls of a growing business.

  • 2017-

    Cloud Evolution

    We are cloud specialists, if you are thinking on migrating, adopt fully managed cloud services, go serverless and, create and build your cloud infrastructure talk to us. Embrace the time-to-market, velocity, elasticity, resilience and provision of resourses that was never possible before.
